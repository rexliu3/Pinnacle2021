What are some tips to stay safe when walking?

Use Sentri to map out the safest route before departing! Avoid wearing headphones or earbuds and always keep alert of your surroundings: no phones.

What should I do if I feel unsafe?
Call for help at 9-1-1 or the emergency service number in your area.

Any other tips?
To learn more, follow these links: https://www.optisafe.in/blog/7-tips-to-stay-safe-while-walking-alone/

Where does the data displayed on map come from?
Data is taken from the FBI servers and is fully verified before shown of map.

How is Sentri different from Google Maps?
While Google Maps only takes into account the fastest route to your destination, Sentri uses a complex algorithm to calculate the safest route, avoiding high crime areas!

When should I use Sentri
Always use Sentri to stay safe, especially when walking alone or at night time.
